## 🎨 Design System Applied

### Spacing
**Normal spacing throughout:**
- Mobile: `py-16` (64px)
- Tablet: `py-20` (80px)
- Desktop: `py-24` (96px)

### Colors
- **Primary Navy:** #001F3F
- **Primary Teal:** #0A5F5F
- **Primary Gold:** #C9A961
- **Neutral Scale:** 50-900

### Backgrounds
- White sections: `bg-white`
- Gradient sections: `bg-gradient-to-br from-neutral-50 to-white`
- Footer: `bg-gradient-to-br from-primary-navy via-[#003366] to-primary-teal`

### Typography
- **Display Font:** Playfair Display (headings)
- **Body Font:** Inter (body text)
- **Fluid sizing:** clamp() functions for responsive text

### Components
- **Premium Cards:** White background, border, hover shadow
- **Feature Cards:** Gradient background, left border accent
- **Buttons:** Primary (gradient), Secondary (outline), Ghost (underline)

